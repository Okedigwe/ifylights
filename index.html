About Ify Lights Stage and Studio


Our Story
At Ify Lights Stage and Studio,
we believe that lighting is more than just visibility
it is the heartbeat of every great performance. Based on our passion for the Entertainment Technology sector, we have dedicated ourselves to providing the professional-grade equipment that transforms a simple stage into an immersive experience.

What We Do
We specialise in high-end lighting solutions tailored for both Stage and Studio environments. Our inventory is curated for those who refuse to compromise on visual impact. Whether you are illuminating a West End theatre production, a high-intensity music festival, or a sleek television studio, we provide the tools to make your vision shine.
Our collection includes cutting-edge technology, such as our multi-head moving beam fixtures. These advanced units feature multiple independently articulated heads and integrated LED rings, designed to create complex, choreographed aerial effects and high-intensity beams that cut through the darkness with absolute precision.

Why Choose Us?
Industry Expertise: We understand the technical demands of the live event and broadcast worlds.
Precision Technology: From DMX-controlled moving heads to cinematic studio washes, our gear is built for reliability.
Versatility: Our equipment is designed to excel in various settings, from the fast-paced energy of a nightclub to the controlled lighting of a professional studio.
